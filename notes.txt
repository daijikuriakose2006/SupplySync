1. PROJECT ABSTRACTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Project Title   : SupplySync - Inventory & Demand Forecasting System
Live URL        : https://supply-sync-tau.vercel.app
Repository      : daijikuriakose2006/SupplySync

OVERVIEW:
---------
SupplySync is a full-stack web-based inventory and demand forecasting system
designed for small to medium-scale retail businesses. The system provides
real-time stock tracking, sales management, demand forecasting using historical
data, GST management, and barcode-based product handling. It supports
multi-role access (Admin and Staff) and allows businesses to manage their
complete supply chain operations through a single integrated platform.

PURPOSE:
--------
The primary goal of SupplySync is to digitize and streamline inventory
management processes, reduce manual errors, automate low-stock alerts, enable
data-driven restocking decisions through demand forecasting, and ensure
compliance with GST (Goods and Services Tax) regulations.

KEY MODULES:
------------
  a) Dashboard        - Real-time summary of sales, stock levels, and alerts.
  b) Inventory        - Add, update, delete, and search products with
                        category filters and barcode support.
  c) Barcode Scanner  - Scan and identify products using barcode system.
  d) Sales (Sell)     - Point-of-sale interface to bill customers, apply
                        GST rates, and add bill charges.
  e) Sales Report     - View and analyze past sales transactions with filters.
  f) Demand Forecast  - Predict future stock requirements based on sales trends.
  g) Recommendations  - Suggest restocking quantities for products.
  h) Alerts           - Notify users about low-stock or out-of-stock items.
  i) GST Management   - Configure and manage GST rates for different products.
  j) Bill Charges     - Add custom charges (e.g., delivery, packing) to bills.
  k) Staff Management - Admin can add, manage, and control staff access.
  l) Settings         - Configure shop/business profile and preferences.

USER ROLES:
-----------
  - Admin  : Full access to all modules including staff and settings.
  - Staff  : Limited access to inventory, sales, and alerts.

TARGET USERS:
-------------
  Small and medium retail shop owners, warehouse managers, and business
  operators who need a lightweight yet powerful inventory control tool.
